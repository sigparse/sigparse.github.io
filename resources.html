Software

Phrase-structure parsing

  • BLLIP Resources, including the Charniak (2010) top-down parser, and the Charniak (2000) parser and Charniak and Johnson (2005) parse reranker.

  • Dan Bikel's parser, including Collins parser emulation.

  • The Berkeley Parser, developed mainly by Slav Petrov.

Dependency parsing

  • MSTParser, developed by Ryan McDonald and currently maintained by Jason Baldridge and Ryan McDonald.

  • MaltParser, developed by Johan Hall, Jens Nilsson and Joakim Nivre.

  • TurboParser, developed by Andre Martins.

  • RBGParser, developed by Tao Lei and Yuan Zhang.

Deep parsing

  • C&C parser, the Clark and Curran CCG parser.

  • Enju, a data-driven HPSG parser from the University of Tokyo's Tsujii Lab.

Data

  • The Universal Dependency Treebank

  • The Copenhagen Dependency Treebank
